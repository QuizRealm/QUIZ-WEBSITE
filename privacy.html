DATA SOVEREIGNTY

Transparency Report regarding user data, storage protocols, and tracking methods.

STATUS: ENCRYPTED // LAST UPDATED: 2024

1. Core Philosophy

QuizRealm is built on a "Privacy-First" architecture. We believe that educational and entertainment platforms should not require personal data to function. We do not require registration, email addresses, or phone numbers to access any part of our service.

02. Local Storage Protocol

Instead of storing your game progress on a central server, we utilize your browser's Local Storage API. This means:

  • Your XP, Levels, and Achievements live physically on your device.
  • We (QuizRealm developers) cannot see, access, or delete your save file.
  • Deleting your browser cache or cookies will erase your progress permanently.

03. Cookies & Third-Party Data

QuizRealm itself does not use tracking cookies. However, we may partner with third-party vendors (such as Google AdSense) who use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

04. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our services are not directed to persons under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

05. Contact & Rights

Although we do not hold your personal data, you have the right to inquire about our privacy practices. If you have questions regarding this policy, you may contact us via our Secure Contact Form.
