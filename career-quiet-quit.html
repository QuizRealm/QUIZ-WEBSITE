Not stress, but emptiness. Most people know Burnout (too much work), but few identify "Boreout" (too little meaning). It creates the same exhaustion symptoms—lethargy, irritability, depression—but stems from being chronically under-challenged.

The Sunday

Anticipatory Anxiety Spike. Why does 4:00 PM on Sunday feel like a funeral? It is a biological response known as the "Sunday Scaries." Your body releases cortisol in anticipation of Monday's threats.

If your job triggers a Fight-or-Flight response when you aren't even there, your workplace is technically a "predator" to your nervous system. You aren't just "hating Mondays"; you are suffering from chronic vigilance.
