The 6-Month Prune

Subject: Quality over Quantity. If a funnel page has 0 views after 6 months, you have three options: **Improve, Merge, or Delete.** Google rewards sites that "clean house." Merging three low-performing "Word Game" funnels into one "Ultimate Word Game Guide" creates a more powerful SEO signal than three separate dead pages.

Internal Power-Linking

Subject: Passing "Link Juice." A page with zero views is often just a "lonely" page. Ensure your high-traffic pages link down to your new funnels. Google needs to see a clear path from your Arcade Home to these 100 pages. If they aren't linked, they are "Orphan Pages," which Google usually ignores or de-indexes.

Selective Indexing

Subject: Protecting the Domain. If you are experimenting with "risky" or highly niche funnels, use the <meta name="robots" content="noindex"> tag. This allows the page to exist for social media ads or direct links without affecting your Google Search Console health. Only "index" the pages that are truly high-quality.

The "Interaction" Requirement

Subject: Human vs. Bot traffic. Google and AdSense both look at "Time on Page." To keep your score high, ensure every funnel has a **"Lead-in" Interaction** (like a mini-poll or a "Start Test" button) before the ads appear. This proves to the system that the user is real and engaged, protecting your AdSense CPC.
