The Science of the Earworm

Why does "All I Want for Christmas is You" return to #1 on the charts every single year? Musicologists point to the "Wall of Sound" production technique and a specific chord progression (shifting from major to minor) that mimics the structure of 1960s Motown hits. It feels nostalgic even the first time you hear it.

Most Streamed

Mariah Carey earns an estimated $2.5 million per year from royalties on that single song alone.

Oldest Hit
