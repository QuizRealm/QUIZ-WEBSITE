Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in this monthly payment?

Our "True Cost" calculator includes Principal, Interest, Property Taxes, Homeowners Insurance, and HOA fees. Most calculators only show Principal and Interest (P&I), which can be misleading.

How much does an extra payment help?

Adding even $100/month specifically towards principal can cut years off a 30-year mortgage. Use the "Extra Monthly Payment" field above to see exact savings instantly.

What is a good Debt-to-Income (DTI) ratio?

Lenders generally prefer a DTI below 36%, with no more than 28% of that debt going towards your mortgage/rent payment.
