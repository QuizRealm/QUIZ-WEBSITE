How does the XP & Leveling system work?

Experience Points (XP) are awarded for every correct answer. You earn bonus XP for maintaining streaks (3+ correct in a row) and completing quizzes on harder difficulties. Leveling up unlocks new profile badges and proves your mastery over the QuizRealm sectors.

Is my progress saved across devices?

Currently, QuizRealm utilizes a Local Neural Storage protocol (Local Storage). Your progress is saved specifically to the device and browser you are using. If you clear your cache or switch devices, your local progress will reset. Cloud Sync accounts are in development for future updates.

What are "Daily Quests"?

Daily Quests are specialized missions that reset every 24 hours. They offer a curated mix of questions from various sectors. Completing them awards double XP and contributes to your "Daily Streak" counter. Don't miss a day, or your streak resets to zero!

How do Power-Ups work?

  • 50/50: Removes two incorrect options, leaving you with a better chance.
  • Time Freeze: Adds +15 seconds to the countdown clock.
  • Skip: Bypasses the current question without breaking your streak (costs 0 points).

What is "Neural Draw"?

Neural Draw is a local multiplayer party mode (similar to Pictionary). One player acts as the "Architect" (drawer) while others try to guess the secret word before the timer expires. It serves as a training module for visual recognition and rapid communication.

How can I report a bug or bad question?

We strive for 100% data accuracy. If you encounter a glitch or an incorrect answer, please contact our support frequency immediately via the contact form linked in the footer. Screenshots are highly appreciated for rapid debugging.
