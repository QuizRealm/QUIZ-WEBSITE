What is Friends?

Friends follows six friends navigating life, love, careers, and chaos in New York City. The show’s format is built for trivia: recurring jokes, relationship arcs, iconic props, and “only true fans notice this” details.

The comedy isn’t just situational — it’s character-driven. That means the best quiz questions often test what each character would do, say, or fail at (spectacularly) in a specific episode.
