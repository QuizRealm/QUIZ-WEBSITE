Ricks

The Central Finite Curve

The multiverse is infinite, but Rick Sanchez didn't want to deal with infinite geniuses. He built the Central Finite Curve—a walled-off subset of infinite realities where Rick is the smartest man in the universe.

Outside this curve lies true chaos: universes where Ricks are dumb, where Ricks are suppressed, or where things far scarier than Rick exist. Evil Morty's grand plan was simply to break this crib and escape to the true multiverse.

Family & Foes

Rick Sanchez (C-137)
The "Rickest Rick." Cynical, alcoholic, god-like intelligence. Driven by the murder of his wife Diane by Rick Prime.
Morty Smith (Prime)
Rick's grandson from the "Prime" universe. Unlike other Mortys, he is becoming independent and dangerous.
Evil Morty
A Morty tired of being a sidekick. He hacked the Citadel, destroyed the Curve, and holds the Golden Portal Gun.
Jerry Smith
Unemployed, beekeeping enthusiast. Somehow survives cosmic horrors through sheer incompetence and cowardice.
Space Beth
The "cool" version of Beth who fights the Federation. It is still unknown if she is the clone or the original.
Mr. Poopybutthole
Long-time family friend. Suffered a tragic karate-chop accident. Ooh-wee!

Tech & Locations

The Portal Gun

Rick's signature tool. It utilizes localized fluid to rip holes in spacetime, allowing instantaneous travel between dimensions. Green portals are within the Curve; Gold portals go beyond.

The Citadel of Ricks

A massive space station where Ricks and Mortys from thousands of realities formed a government. It has been destroyed and rebuilt multiple times. Capital of the bureaucracy Rick hates.

Meeseeks Box

A gadget that summons a blue humanoid named Mr. Meeseeks. Their singular purpose is to complete one task and then vanish. "Existence is pain" for a Meeseeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Rick Prime dead?
Yes. In Season 7, Rick C-137 finally hunted down and killed Rick Prime (the one who killed Diane), ending his lifelong quest for revenge but leaving him empty.
What does "Wubba Lubba Dub Dub" mean?
In Birdperson's language, it translates to: "I am in great pain, please help me."
What is the "Omega Device"?
A weapon built by Rick Prime capable of erasing someone from *every* conceivable universe simultaneously. It was used to erase Diane Sanchez from existence.
