What is The Office (US)?

The Office (US) is a mockumentary-style workplace comedy following employees at Dunder Mifflin’s Scranton branch. The format turns everyday office life into comedy through interviews, awkward pauses, character quirks, and unexpectedly emotional moments.

For trivia, it’s perfect: the show is packed with recurring gags, callbacks, nicknames, party themes, pranks, and scene details hidden in the background.
