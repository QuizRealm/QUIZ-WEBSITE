What is the MCU?

The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is an American media franchise and shared universe centered on a series of superhero films produced by Marvel Studios. It isn't just a series of movies; it's a serialized narrative told on the biggest screen possible.

Starting with Iron Man in 2008, the saga weaves together individual hero stories into massive crossover events like The Avengers and Infinity War. If you miss a detail in one movie, it might be the key to understanding another one three years later.

Frequently asked questions

What is the "Sacred Timeline"?
It refers to the main flow of time in the MCU (Earth-616). Deviations from this create the Multiverse, which is the focus of Phases 4, 5, and 6.
What order should I watch them in?
You can watch by Release Order (how audiences saw them) or Chronological Order (Captain America first, Captain Marvel second, etc.).
